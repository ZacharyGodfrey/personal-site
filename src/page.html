Career

My secret weapon is my ability to explain complex technical concepts in simple terms for non-technical people. The less my work gets called magic the better! I love bridging the gap between developers and non-developers because I've seen how much my teams benefit when everyone has a better understanding of what we do.

When it comes to code, I think the best quality metric is how easy the code is to understand. Efficiency is great too, but my main concern is the maintainability of the software. When code is easy to read and follow, it's easier to find and fix bugs and teams can onboard new members more quickly.
